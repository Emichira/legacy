Company Structure

Legacy systems ltd is comprised of three into three major departments:

Legacy Systems Company

This is the mother company that solicits for projects that are tackled by the legacy systems hub.

Legacy Systems Academy

This is a professional training facility that equips IT personnel with hands-on skills that are required in the market. The academy aims at bridging the gap between actual market demands in terms of practical skill set and what is supplied from learning institutions.

Legacy Systems Hub

This is an incubator that comprises a team of information technology professionals that have been extensively trained by the legacy systems academy. These professionals work on projects sourced by and tendered to legacy Systems Company.

Our Story

Legacy systems ltd was established in the year 2015 as IT firm to provide product and services such as: supply of computers and related accessories, web design and development, application design and development, networking, database management, system security, software and hardware maintenance among others; however due to the changing technology environment and the growing needs of our Clients; it became necessary to broaden the services that could be provided by Legacy systems ltd, so as to offer multi-disciplinary software development training services service under Legacy Academy i.e. (web design and development, graphic design, desktop application design and development) and incubation hub for the trainees under one banner. Legacy systems ltd has added to its portfolio a full-time IT Programmer for custom-built Management Information Systems (MIS) and other software applications.

Legacy systems ltd has continued to strive towards becoming a complete multi-disciplinary practice offering our Clients the specialist individual attention and solutions required by an ever-changing market and IT environment. Legacy systems ltd views teamwork on every project as the key success element and is responsible for creating this environment. The over-all project manager is duty-bound in ensuring efficient and regular communication to all stakeholders and at all levels.

Legacy system believes in a hands-on approach on all projects. That is why a Director of the firm will always be in control of the key functions on our projects. Legacy systems ltd is equipped with the latest technology and has the necessary staff and resources to ensure the best professional service is provided at all times.
